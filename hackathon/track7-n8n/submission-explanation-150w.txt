This workflow turns raw citizen dispute text into a structured legal triage decision using NYAN.AI APIs. It starts with a webhook trigger, validates and normalizes the request, and calls NYAN intake to extract a clean dispute record. If the intake agent needs clarification, the workflow returns targeted follow-up questions immediately. Otherwise, it runs legal analysis and checks success probability to make an adaptive decision: low-probability disputes are routed for urgent human legal review, while stronger cases are marked ready for document generation. This creates a practical agentic loop where the system asks questions when uncertain and escalates when risk is high. The output is a consistent JSON decision object with probability, recommendation, and the enriched dispute record, making it easy to plug into UI, notifications, or downstream legal operations. The design is modular, production-safe, and focused on real-world legal accessibility impact.
